Positive and Negative Impacts of Technology


Technology has a positive and harmaful impact on society. On the plus side, technology has helped societies increase productivity, expand service accessibility, and boost overall well-being. For example, technology has enabled healthcare institutions to become more efficent, while telemedicine apps have made healthcare more equal and accessible to all, regardless of socioeconomic situation. Similarly, technology has made educatiion more accessible and scalable than ever before, with several learning platforms aggregating courses for different age groups, as well as from other areas and sectors.

On the other hand, technology has had certain detrimental effects on society. Social media, in particular, has been condemned for spreading disinformation, hate speech, and harassment, as well as for isolating and depressing people. It's vital to remember that technology has the potential to make our lives both easier and more difficult. It all relies on how we use it.
